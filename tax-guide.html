SmartWerk Tax Report Guide

Note: This report is not an official tax ruling from Belastingdienst. It serves as a helper summary to assist freelancers and small businesses in the Netherlands with submitting their tax declaration manually.

How to use

  • Use the calculator to estimate your taxes for a given period (month or full calendar year).
  • Click “Download for Belastingdienst” to get a PDF report.
  • This PDF can be shared with your accountant or used as a support document when submitting your declaration.

What's included in the report

  • Gross Income — Total earnings before tax (hours × hourly rate).
  • Total Expenses — Business-related deductions that reduce your taxable profit.
  • ZZP Deductions — Special tax deductions for self-employed (zelfstandigenaftrek & startersaftrek).
  • MKB Vrijstelling — Small business exemption (12.7% of remaining profit after ZZP deductions).
  • Final Taxable Income — The amount you are taxed on.
  • Income Tax — Based on progressive Dutch income tax brackets.
  • ZVW — Healthcare contribution (5.32%).
  • BTW — VAT to be paid unless KOR exemption is used.
  • Net Income — Your final income after all taxes are subtracted.

Important notes

  • If KOR (kleineondernemersregeling) is applied, your BTW (VAT) is set to €0.00 in the report.
  • Make sure the period (e.g., January–December 2025) is filled out clearly in the form before exporting the PDF.
  • All values are shown in euros (€) and rounded to two decimal places.
