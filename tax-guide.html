SmartWerk Tax Declaration Guide

⚠️ This is not an official tax ruling.
This document serves as a support tool for preparing and submitting your declaration to the Belastingdienst (Dutch Tax Authority).

📅 Period Covered

This report is based on the data you entered for the selected period. Please ensure the Period field in the calculator is correct:

  • 01-01-2025 to 31-12-2025 (Full Calendar Year)
  • or quarterly period: Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4

💶 What This Report Includes

  • Gross Income – Total income based on hours × rate
  • Business Expenses – Deductible costs: Vehicle, Office, Internet, Marketing, Other
  • Taxable Profit – Income minus expenses
  • ZZP Deductions – Self-employed tax relief (Zelfstandigenaftrek, Startersaftrek)
  • MKB Exemption – 12.7% discount for small businesses
  • Final Taxable Income – The amount used to calculate income tax
  • Income Tax – Based on Dutch rates (19% up to €200,000; 25.8% above)
  • ZVW – Health insurance contribution (5.32%)
  • BTW (VAT) – Added unless KOR is applied
  • Total Estimated Tax
  • Net Income After Tax
  • KvK Number / IBAN / Contact Info

All values are shown in EUR (€) with two decimal places.

📊 Additional Notes

  • KOR (kleineondernemersregeling): If applied, VAT = €0.00
  • ZZP Deduction: Applied if KvK is valid and minimum 1225 hours confirmed
  • MKB Exemption: Automatically calculated if ZZP eligible

🚤 How to Use This Report

  1. 📧 Share the PDF with your tax advisor or accountant
  2. 📤 Upload it while completing your declaration at:
    • Mijn Belastingdienst Zakelijk
  3. 📋 Use values in:
    • Income Tax Declaration (Inkomstenbelasting)
    • VAT Declaration (BTW-aangifte), if applicable
