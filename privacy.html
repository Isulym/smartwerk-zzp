Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartWerk. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Who We Are

SmartWerk is a digital platform for freelancers and businesses based in the Netherlands. Our official contact email is: smartwerk.team@gmail.com

3. What Data We Collect

  • Personal identification data (name, email, phone number)
  • Business information (KvK number, IBAN, company name)
  • Usage data (browser, IP address, device info)
  • Invoices, contracts, templates you generate
  • AI chat interactions (non-sensitive only)

4. Why We Collect This Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To store and manage your data securely
  • To generate personalized templates and documents
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We only process your personal data when we have a legal basis, including:

  • Consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legal obligation
  • Legitimate interest

6. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your data is stored securely using encryption and access controls. We use secure connections (SSL) and reliable cloud infrastructure. Data is retained only as long as necessary.

7. Third-Party Access

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted third parties only when necessary, such as:

  • Cloud storage providers (e.g., Firebase, Vercel)
  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • AI providers (for document generation)
All third parties are GDPR-compliant.

8. Cookies and Tracking

SmartWerk uses essential and analytics cookies to improve functionality. You will be asked to consent to cookies when required.

9. Your Rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise your rights, contact us at smartwerk.team@gmail.com.

10. Data Transfers Outside the EU

Some of our service providers may be based outside the EU. We ensure they offer equivalent protection through standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions.
